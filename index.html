Frequently Asked Questions

How to check AKTU Result 2026? +

AKTU Result check karne ke liye official AKTU One View portal par jaye: https://oneview.aktu.ac.in. Apna roll number aur date of birth enter karke result dekh sakte hai.

What is SGPA and CGPA in AKTU? +

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) ek semester ka average grade point hai. CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) sabhi semesters ka combined average hai. CGPA = Total of all SGPA / Number of semesters.

How to convert CGPA to percentage? +

AKTU me CGPA ko percentage me convert karne ka formula hai: Percentage = CGPA √ó 9.5. Example: Agar CGPA 8.0 hai to percentage = 8.0 √ó 9.5 = 76%

When will AKTU declare results? +

AKTU usually exam ke 45-60 days baad results declare karta hai. Exact date ke liye official AKTU website check karte rahe ya hamare notification updates follow kare.

What if I fail in AKTU exam? +

Agar kisi subject me fail ho jate hai to carry over/back paper exam de sakte hai. Back paper exam usually next semester ke saath conduct hota hai. Detailed rules ke liye Back Paper Rules page dekhe.
