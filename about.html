About AKTU Results

Welcome to akturesults.in

akturesults.in is an independent student-focused educational information website created to help AKTU (Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University) students access important information, tools, and resources all in one place.

Our Mission

To provide AKTU students with:

  • Quick access to official result portals and academic information
  • Free, easy-to-use calculators (SGPA to CGPA, CGPA to Percentage, Attendance)
  • Comprehensive guides on academic procedures
  • Latest updates on exams, admissions, and important dates
  • Helpful resources for academic success

This platform is managed by Pawan, an AKTU student who understands the challenges students face in finding accurate and timely information. The website was created to make academic life easier for fellow students.
