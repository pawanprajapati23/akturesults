What is UPTAC Counselling?

UPTAC (Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Committee) AKTU aur UP ke technical colleges me admission ke liye counselling conduct karta hai. JEE Main aur other entrance exam ke basis par seats allocate hoti hai.

UPTAC Counselling Process 2026

  1. Registration: UPTAC portal par register kare with JEE rank
  2. Document Verification: Required documents upload kare
  3. Choice Filling: Preferred colleges aur branches choose kare
  4. Choice Locking: Final choices lock kare deadline se pehle
  5. Seat Allotment: Merit ke basis par seat allocate hoti hai
  6. Reporting: Allotted college me documents ke saath report kare
  7. Fee Payment: Admission confirm karne ke liye fee pay kare

Important Documents Required

  • JEE Main Scorecard / Rank Card
  • Class 10th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Class 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Income Certificate (for fee concession)
  • Photo & Signature (scanned)

UPTAC Counselling Rounds

Usually 3-4 rounds hote hai:

  • Round 1: First preference choices ke basis par
  • Round 2: Vacant seats ke liye
  • Round 3: Final round for remaining seats
  • Spot Round: Last minute vacant seats (if any)

Choice Filling Tips

  • Maximum colleges aur branches choose kare to increase chances
  • Priority wise arrange kare (most preferred first)
  • College reputation, location, branch scope sab consider kare
  • Cutoff trends dekh kar realistic choices bhi dalo
  • Lock karne se pehle 2-3 baar verify kar lo

After Seat Allotment

Seat allot hone ke baad:

  • Allotment letter download kare
  • Designated college me reporting date pe jaye
  • Original documents verification ke liye le jaye
  • Admission fee pay kare
  • If not satisfied, wait for next round (but security deposit may apply)
