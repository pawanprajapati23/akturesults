What is AKTU ERP?

AKTU ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) student portal hai jaha students apni academic information access kar sakte hai including results, attendance, fee status, aur important notices.

Available Services on ERP

  • View Results: Semester wise marks aur grades
  • Attendance: Subject wise attendance percentage
  • Fee Status: Paid aur pending fee details
  • Exam Schedule: Upcoming exam dates
  • Admit Card: Download hall ticket
  • Academic Records: Complete academic history
  • Notices: Important announcements

How to Login to AKTU ERP?

  1. Go to https://erp.aktu.ac.in/Login.aspx
  2. Enter your Username (usually roll number)
  3. Enter your Password
  4. Click on "Login" button
  5. Access your dashboard

Forgot Password?

Agar password bhool gaye to:

  • Login page par "Forgot Password" option par click kare
  • Registered email ID enter kare
  • Password reset link email pe aayega
  • Ya college IT department se contact kare

First Time Login?

  • Default username: Your roll number
  • Default password: Usually date of birth (DDMMYYYY)
  • First login me password change karna compulsory hota hai
  • Strong password rakhe for security

Troubleshooting

  • Login Failed: Username/password verify kare
  • Account Locked: Too many wrong attempts, wait or contact support
  • Page Not Loading: Clear browser cache and try again
  • Data Not Showing: Refresh page or try different browser
