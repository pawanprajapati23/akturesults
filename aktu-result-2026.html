How to Check AKTU Result 2026?

AKTU ke students apna result check karne ke liye ye simple steps follow kare:

  1. AKTU One View portal par jaye: https://oneview.aktu.ac.in
  2. Apna Roll Number enter kare
  3. Date of Birth select kare (DD/MM/YYYY format me)
  4. "Submit" button par click kare
  5. Apna result screen par dekh sakte hai
  6. Result ka printout ya screenshot le le for future reference

Important Details on Result

  • SGPA: Semester Grade Point Average - Current semester ka GPA
  • CGPA: Cumulative Grade Point Average - Overall GPA
  • Subject Grades: Individual subject marks and grades
  • Credits Earned: Total credits for the semester
  • Pass/Fail Status: Whether you passed all subjects

Frequently Asked Questions

AKTU result kab aayega? +

AKTU usually exam ke 45-60 days me results declare karta hai. Latest updates ke liye official website check karte rahe.

AKTU result mobile par kaise dekhe? +

Mobile par result dekhne ke liye AKTU One View portal kholo, roll number aur DOB enter karo. Portal mobile-friendly hai.

SGPA aur CGPA me kya difference hai? +

SGPA ek semester ka grade point average hai, jabki CGPA sabhi semesters ka combined average hai.

Result me error hai to kya kare? +

Result me koi error dikhe to turant apne college administration se contact kare. Wo AKTU ke saath issue raise karenge.
