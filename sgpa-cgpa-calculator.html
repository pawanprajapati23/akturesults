How to Calculate CGPA from SGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) sabhi semesters ke SGPA ka average hai. AKTU me CGPA calculate karne ka formula simple hai:

CGPA Formula

Where n = Number of semesters

Example Calculation:

Maan lo aapke semester marks ye hai:

  • 1st Semester SGPA: 7.5
  • 2nd Semester SGPA: 8.0
  • 3rd Semester SGPA: 7.8
  • 4th Semester SGPA: 8.2

CGPA = (7.5 + 8.0 + 7.8 + 8.2) / 4 = 31.5 / 4 = 7.875

Important Points:

  • CGPA is calculated on a 10-point scale
  • Sabhi semesters ka equal weightage hota hai
  • Failed subjects ka grade 0 hota hai (affects CGPA)
  • CGPA se percentage nikalne ke liye multiply by 9.5

Frequently Asked Questions

CGPA kya hota hai? +

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) sabhi semesters ka combined grade point average hai. Ye 10-point scale par calculate hota hai.

SGPA aur CGPA me difference kya hai? +

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) sirf ek semester ka GPA hai, jabki CGPA sabhi semesters ka combined average hai.

Kya back paper CGPA me count hota hai? +

Haan, failed subjects ka grade 0 count hota hai CGPA me. Back paper clear karne ke baad new grade se CGPA recalculate hota hai.
