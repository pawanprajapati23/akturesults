AKTU Back Paper Rules 2026

Complete Guide on Back Paper / Carry Over Exams

1. Eligibility for Back Paper

  • Koi bhi student jo kisi subject me fail ho gaya ho
  • Minimum 40% marks nahi aa paaye theory me
  • Practical me fail ho gaye ho
  • Attendance ke wajah se debarred hue students (special cases me)

2. When to Appear for Back Paper?

Back paper exam regular semester exam ke saath conduct hota hai. For example:

  • 1st sem me fail → 2nd sem exam ke saath back paper
  • 3rd sem me fail → 4th sem exam ke saath back paper

3. Maximum Back Papers Limit

No limit! Jitne subjects me fail ho, utne back papers de sakte hai. Lekin:

  • Sabhi backs clear karne honge degree ke liye
  • Final year me hone se pehle maximum backs clear karna better hai

4. Back Paper Exam Fee

Exam fee varies but approximately:

  • Per subject: ₹250-400 (check latest fee structure)
  • Late fee may apply agar form late submit kare

5. How to Fill Back Paper Form?

  1. College me notice check kare form filling ke dates ke liye
  2. Exam form portal par login kare
  3. Failed subjects select kare
  4. Fee payment kare online
  5. Form submit kare aur print le lo

6. Important Points

  • Back paper clear hone par grade improve hota hai
  • CGPA recalculate hota hai with new marks
  • Degree ke liye sabhi backs clear hone chahiye
  • Placement me backs ka impact padta hai
  • Regular classes attend karte rahe, back paper ke saath

7. Tips to Clear Back Papers

  • Syllabus properly cover kare
  • Previous year papers practice kare
  • Faculty se help lo difficult topics me
  • Study group join kare
  • Time management important hai
