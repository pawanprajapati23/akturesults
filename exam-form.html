AKTU Exam Form 2026

How to Fill AKTU Examination Form?

Har semester me exam dene ke liye examination form bharna compulsory hai. Form college ya online portal se submit hota hai.

üìù GO TO EXAM FORM PORTAL

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Check Notice: College notice board ya website se form filling dates check kare
  2. Login: Student portal me login kare with credentials
  3. Fill Details: Personal aur academic details verify kare
  4. Subject Selection: Current semester subjects select kare
  5. Back Papers: Agar koi back paper hai to wo bhi add kare
  6. Upload Documents: Required documents scan karke upload kare
  7. Fee Payment: Online fee payment kare
  8. Submit: Final submission ke baad receipt save kare
  9. Print: Form printout le kar college me submit kare

Required Documents

  • Passport size photo (scanned)
  • Signature (scanned)
  • Previous semester marksheet (for continuing students)
  • Fee receipt (if applicable)

Important Points

  • Form bharne ki deadline strictly follow kare
  • Late submission me extra fee lagti hai
  • Bina form bhare exam me nahi baith sakte
  • All details carefully verify kare before submit
  • Receipt safely save kar ke rakhe

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wrong subject selection
  • Incorrect personal details
  • Poor quality photo/signature upload
  • Incomplete fee payment
  • Missing deadline

What if Form Submission Failed?

  • College examination cell se immediately contact kare
  • Payment receipt dikha ke issue resolve kare
  • Technical problems ke liye IT department se help le
