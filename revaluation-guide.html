AKTU Revaluation Guide 2026

What is Revaluation?

Revaluation ya Rechecking me aapki answer sheet dobara check hoti hai. Agar aapko lagta hai ki marking me error hua hai to aap revaluation ke liye apply kar sakte hai.

When to Apply for Revaluation?

  • Jab expected marks se bahut kam aaye
  • Fail ho gaye ho lekin exam achha diya tha
  • Just pass ho gaye ho (40-45 marks)
  • Border marks pe ho (69, 79, 89 etc.)

Revaluation Process Step-by-Step

  1. Check Result Date: Result aane ke baad notice dekhe revaluation dates ke liye
  2. Apply Online: AKTU portal par revaluation form bhare (usually 7-10 days window hoti hai)
  3. Select Subjects: Jin subjects me revaluation chahiye wo select kare
  4. Pay Fee: Per subject fee pay kare (approx ₹500-700)
  5. Submit Form: Application submit kar ke receipt save kare
  6. Wait for Result: Revaluation result 15-30 days me aata hai

Types of Revaluation

1. Rechecking/Revaluation: Answer sheet dobara check hoti hai total marks ke liye

2. Photocopy of Answer Sheet: Apni answer sheet ki copy mil sakti hai (separate fee)

3. Challenge Revaluation: After photocopy dekh kar agar confirm issues ho to ye option hai

Important Points

  • Revaluation me marks increase, decrease ya same reh sakte hai
  • Only totaling aur marking scheme check hota hai, not subjective evaluation
  • Fee refund nahi hota agar marks nahi badhte
  • Multiple subjects ke liye alag-alag apply karna padta hai

Success Rate

Generally:

  • 20-30% cases me marks improve hote hai
  • Maximum improvement 5-10 marks tak ho sakti hai
  • Border cases me chances jyada hote hai

Should You Apply?

Apply karne se pehle ye consider kare:

  • Kya exam performance really achhi thi?
  • Kya border marks pe ho?
  • Kya financial investment worth hai?
  • Kya marks improvement se practical benefit hoga?
