AKTU Back Paper Result 2026

What is Back Paper?

Back Paper ya Carry Over wo exam hai jo students fail hone ke baad dete hai. Agar kisi subject me pass nahi ho paye to uska back paper next semester ke saath de sakte hai.

CHECK BACK PAPER RESULT

How to Check Back Paper Result?

  1. AKTU One View portal par jaye
  2. Roll number aur DOB enter kare
  3. Result me carry over subjects ka marks dekhe
  4. Pass/Fail status check kare

Important Information

  • Back paper exam regular exam ke saath conduct hota hai
  • Jitne subjects me fail ho, utne back papers de sakte hai
  • Koi maximum limit nahi hai back papers ki
  • Pass hone par CGPA me improvement hota hai
